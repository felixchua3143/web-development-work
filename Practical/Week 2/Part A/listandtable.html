Singapore Healthy Cooking Competition 2016

Rules and Regulations

  • Competators are given 20mins to shop for ingredients at NTUC next to YCK community center.
  • Total price of ingredients should not be more than $10
  • Each team is to prepare
    1. One pot of Soup
    2. 2 Dishes
  • Total time spent for preparation and cooking should not exceed an hour
  • Judges decision is final
